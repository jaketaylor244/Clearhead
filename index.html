About Clearhead
Clearhead uses CBT and DBT techniques to help you challenge distorted thoughts, work through OCD and anxiety, process emotions, and track patterns. A quiet Christ-centered voice (NLT) is woven in only when it genuinely fits. Not a replacement for a real therapist or pastor — a companion between sessions.

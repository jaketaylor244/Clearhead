About Clearhead
Clearhead uses CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) and DBT (Dialectical Behavior Therapy) techniques to help you challenge distorted thoughts, work through OCD and anxiety, process emotions, and track patterns. It's a tool — not a replacement for a real therapist, but a good companion between sessions.
